# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analytics & Forecasting Project

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales data to generate business insights, identify customer behavior patterns, forecast future sales, and recommend data-driven business strategies.

The project simulates real-world analytics tasks performed by companies like Amazon, Flipkart, and Meesho to improve sales performance, customer retention, and business growth.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze monthly and yearly sales trends
- Identify top-selling products and categories
- Perform geographic sales analysis
- Segment customers using RFM Analysis
- Forecast future sales using Time Series Forecasting
- Identify business opportunities and growth strategies
- Create professional business reports and presentations

---

- Scikit-learn
